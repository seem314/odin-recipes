Mini apple Pies

hand held pies

Do you like apple pies but you are in a hurry ? in this recipe we will show you how to make a mini apple pies without the hassles of eating one

Ingredients

  • ¾ stick cold butter (6 tablespoons) cut into tablespoon pieces
  • 4 ounces cold cream cheese cut into tablespoon pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Instructions

Apple Filling

  1. Brown the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Once it is golden and toasty, stir in the sugars, salt, cinnamon and vanilla.
  2. Then stir in the peeled, cubed apple pieces into the warm caramel mixture.
  3. Zest the lemon and cook over medium heat for about 8-10 minutes until the apples are softened. Set aside to cool.

Assembly

  1. Roll dough out until 1/4” thick place and cut into 8 apple shapes or rectangles (~3-4 inches in length)
  2. Scoop 2 tablespoons of the apple mixture into the center of 4 pieces of the dough
  3. Make the egg wash by mixing together the milk and egg. Brush egg wash along the outside edge of the crust where there is no apple filling
  4. Place the 4 pieces without apple on top of the pieces with apple and egg wash. Seal them by pressing a fork along the outside edge of the pie so both pieces of crust stick together
  5. Brush egg wash across the entire top of the hand pie and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar
  6. Cut a small slit or poke a few holes across the top of the pie
  7. Bake at 375 degrees F for 30 minutes until golden
